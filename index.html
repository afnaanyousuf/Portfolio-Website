PROJECT 2 - AI ON HRM

View Description

The rapid advancement of Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies has brought about significant transformations across various industries, and human resource management (HRM) is no exception. This descriptive research study aims to explore and analyze the impact of AI on HRM practices within organizations. Through a comprehensive examination of existing literature, case studies, and empirical evidence, this paper seeks to shed light on the ways in which AI is influencing HR functions, processes, and strategies. The findings of this study will contribute to a better understanding of the opportunities, challenges, and implications of integrating AI into HRM practices, ultimately guiding organizations in leveraging AI technologies effectively to enhance HR outcomes and organizational performance
